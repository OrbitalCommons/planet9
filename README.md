# Planet 9

Research and analysis related to the hypothesized Planet Nine in the outer Solar System.

## Background

Planet Nine is a hypothetical large planet in the far outer Solar System, proposed to explain the unusual clustering of orbits for a group of extreme trans-Neptunian objects (ETNOs). The hypothesis was put forward by Michael E. Brown and Konstantin Batygin at Caltech in 2016.
